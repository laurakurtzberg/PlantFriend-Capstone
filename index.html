Plant Friend

Data and friendship help you take care of your plants

Data and Art

Use both your analytic and your artistic sides to take better care of your houseplants, engaging your interests in both information design and gardening.

Responsive

The Plant Friend web app is responsive, so it can be used on any device with a web browser. This includes desktop computers, laptops, tablets and mobile phones.

Affordable

The seeed brand sensors and the Wio link wifi board cost $25 all together. That is all you need to set up a plant friend of your own!

Plants for Your Health

Maintain all your plants alive and healthy, and increase your own well being as a result. Having green air-purifying plants around you can improve your health and mood.

Personality

Fun personalization of each plant friend infuses the application with personality and fun. Each plant is represented as a unique illustration!

Try it Yourself

All the code for Plant Friend is freely available on Github, allowing anyone to replicate the project for their very own plant!
